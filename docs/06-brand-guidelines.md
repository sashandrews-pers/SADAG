## Voice & Tone
> How should the writing on this site sound?

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Warm and empathetic | Cold or clinical |
| Clear and simple language | Jargon-heavy |
| Hopeful and encouraging | Alarmist or dramatic |
| Inclusive of all South Africans | Exclusive or assumed audience |

## Imagery Guidelines
- Use diverse, authentic South African people and communities
- Avoid stock photos that feel generic or foreign
- Prefer natural lighting and warm tones
- Never use distressing or triggering imagery
- All images must have descriptive alt text
